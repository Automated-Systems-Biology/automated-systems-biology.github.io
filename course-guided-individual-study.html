Summary

The student works on an individual topic with a chosen supervisor. This individual work may lead to a TDK (Students' Scientific Association) paper, an independent research project, etc. Guided Individual Study is not tied to a specific course in the curriculum; its content is based on an individual agreement between the supervisor and the student, and the credit awarded is determined only at the end of the semester based on performance.
