## Course summary

The student carries out independent research that prepares them for writing their thesis. They become familiar with the specific characteristics of the given research field/topic, review the relevant literature, learn the laboratory and/or computational techniques and applications to be used, and where applicable conduct preliminary experiments. Their work is continuously supervised by their advisor, and at the end of the semester they report on the completed work in writing and orally before a committee.
