Introduction

Research & teaching

The Automated Systems Biology group develops automated, robotics-driven workflows for cell biology — combining liquid-handling robots, high-content optical microscopy and computational analysis to study living systems at scale and with high reproducibility. Alongside research, the lab contributes to graduate teaching at PPKE's Faculty of Information Technology and Bionics.

Recent work includes an automated interaction-analysis workflow that quantifies competition in mixed yeast colony biofilms from time-lapse imaging, and open-source tools for 3D co-expression analysis in fluorescence microscopy. The laboratory is led by Dr. Csaba I. Pongor (ORCID 0000-0002-6820-1285), bridging physical chemistry, super-resolution imaging and laboratory automation.

Instrumentation

An automated, integrated platform

From genomics-grade liquid handlers to fully motorized confocal microscopes, our instruments are designed to interoperate in automated end-to-end workflows.

Laboratory Automation

Hamilton Microstar Starlet

Hamilton Starlet is a liquid handling robot equipped with 4 automated pipettes, CoRe Grippers and iSwap robotic gripper. The system is fully equipped for aseptic work as it has a hepa filter for laminar flow and UV for sterilization. It is also equipped with heating, shaking and ultrasonic unit.

Tecan Freedom EVO 200

The Freedom EVO from TECAN is a liquid handling platform equipped with two 8 channel pipetting head and a robotic gripper. It is also equipped with heating, shaking and magnetic isolation units

Opentrons FLEX

The OpenTrons Flex liquid handling work is a full genomics workstation. Equipped with several single and 8 channel pipetting heads, automated PCR instrument, heating and magnetic isolation units.

Spectrophotometry

TS

Tecan Spark

This is a multimode platereader for multimode platereader. This spectrophotometer can be used for UV/VIS (200–1,000 nm) and Fluorescent mode using its integrated monochromator (230–900 nm). It is also equipped with a humidity cassette and a cooling unit (18°C to 42°C). The system can be integrated with liquid handling robots for fully automated workflows.

Microscopy

NE

Nikon Eclipse Ti2-U

Standard widefield fluorescent microscope with a motorized stage, a DIC system and a full array of plan apo objectives (4x, 20x, 60X).

Nikon Eclipse Ti2-E

Fully motorized fluorescent microscope for widefield and confocal imaging. It is motorized in XY and Z and is fitted with a motorized filtercube and objective turret. The microscope is equipped with a spinning disc confocal camera from Crest Optics with a Photometrics Prime camera. As the microscope has a large field of view (25mm) and thus is also equipped with a high sensitivity Photometrics Kinetix camera. The system is automated thanks to the Nikon NIS Elements Jobs module which enables smart imaging. The system is also equipped with a full plan apo objective series (2x, 4x, 20x and 60x). This system is also surrounded by an OkoLab incubator for live cell imaging.
